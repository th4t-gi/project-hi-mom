### What techniques did you use to discover solutions to users' needs?

The primary technique that we used to figure out users' needs was through interviews asking how people found events on campus, what calendar management system they used, etc. Another technique that we used was analyzing what places/tools are already in place for campus events. These included events.grinnell.edu, old fashioned bulletin boards, and the grinnell college instagram story to name a few. 


### What solution(s) did you ultimate pursue and why did you choose them?

Ultimately, we decided to pursue a system that is as automated and as little work from the user's perspective as possible because of the already crowded space we were going into. Grant I believe had an idea of integrating the grinnell events directly into people's digital calendars since so many people use Google or Apple calendar, which fit our constrait of a seemless user experience. So the only input that we would need from the user is what events do they want to hear about and when.
