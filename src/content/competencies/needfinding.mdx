### What techniques did you employ to discover users' needs?

The first technique we used was analyzing what features and information already exists on digital campus calendars like events.grinnell.edu, the grinnell college instagram story, and 25Live, and we took note of what features we liked and what features we didn't think were beneficial. Next, we interviewed people and asked primarily how people found events on campus, what calendar management system they used, and what they liked/disliked about the already existing tools mentioned previously.


### What did you learn about users' needs from this process?

We learned that students most of the time heard about campus events through word of mouth or on flyers, and did want some sort of central place for this information. Around half of the people I talked to didn't know that events.grinnell.edu already existed, and the other half didn't use it because it was inconvenient to filter/sort. This lead us to ensure that our interface was simple and straightforward to use.
